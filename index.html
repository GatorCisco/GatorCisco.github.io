About Me

I am a believer of a better way. In my experiences as a student, engineer and tour manager I have always tried to be an innovator. Even though I don't always succeed I am always striving for a faster and more efficient way to complete the task at hand.
After earning my bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Florida I was able to travel to 46 states as a marketing tour manager. I lead multiple health and wellness programs, directing thousands of participants to a healthier lifestyle as I explored this great country.
Wanting to leave the nomadic lifestyle, I enrolled in Iron Hack’s UX/UI bootcamp in Miami. I am striving to become a user-centered designer, with the intention to create enhanced experiential solutions that are derived from the findings of sound research.
